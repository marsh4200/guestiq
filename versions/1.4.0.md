## v1.4.0 — Overdue checkout alerts, stay timeline & room QR lockout

### Overdue checkout notifications
- Background watcher (60 s) flags any guest still checked in past their
  check-out time and raises an alert.
- Alerts appear as a red banner on the Check-ins tab, a red **Overdue checkout**
  stat, a red row + `Overdue 3h 20m` pill in the in-house table, and are counted
  in the nav badge.
- Home Assistant notification fired for every alert — `persistent_notification.create`
  in token mode, or the webhook payload `{"event": "overdue_checkout", …}` as a
  fallback. Uses the existing Automation tab connection.
- Configurable in **Settings → Overdue checkout alerts**: on/off, grace period
  (default 15 min) and re-notify interval (default every 6 h, `0` = notify once).
- `Check for overdue now` button, plus `POST /api/alerts/scan` to sweep on demand.
- **Extend** button on overdue rows pushes the due-out time out (+2 h / +1 day
  shortcuts) and clears the alert instead of forcing a checkout.

### Stay timeline on checkout
- New `stays.checked_out_at` column records the *actual* checkout time
  (previously only the *planned* one was stored).
- Checkout modal now shows the full picture before you confirm: checked in →
  due out → checking out now → on time / overstayed by X → stay length.
- After confirming, a receipt modal shows checked in, due out, **checked out**
  and the overstay.
- New **Recent departures** card lists the last 12 checkouts with checked-in,
  due-out, actual checked-out and overstay per guest.
- Check-ins tab auto-refreshes every 60 s so overdue guests surface without a
  manual reload.

### Room QR lockout
- Scanning a room QR after the guest has checked out no longer shows Wi-Fi
  credentials, the bar / restaurant menu, or stay details — it shows a
  "stay ended, please contact reception" page with reception and emergency
  numbers only.
- The printed QR code stays valid: it goes dark while the room is empty and
  comes back automatically when the next guest is checked in.
- Configurable in **Settings → Room QR access**: on/off, grace period after
  checkout (default 0 min) and the message shown on the locked page.

### Time handling fix
- Check-in / check-out timestamps were written in UTC while the admin UI's
  date pickers send local wall-clock, so stored times were 2 h out and overdue
  comparisons would have fired late. All timestamps are now stamped in local
  time via **Settings → Time zone offset from UTC** (default `120` = UTC+2).
- Existing rows are left as-is; only new timestamps use the corrected clock.

### API
- `GET  /api/stays` — now returns `checked_out_at`, `overdue`, `minutes_over`,
  `overdue_text`, `overstayed_minutes`, `overstayed_text`, `duration_text`,
  and accepts `?limit=`
- `GET  /api/stays/overdue`
- `POST /api/stays/{id}/extend`
- `GET  /api/alerts` · `POST /api/alerts/{id}/ack` · `POST /api/alerts/ack-all`
  · `POST /api/alerts/scan`
- `GET  /api/room/{code}` — now returns `locked: true` with a reduced payload
  once the room is vacant

### Database
Additive migrations only, applied automatically on start:
`stays.checked_out_at`, `stays.overdue_notified_at`, new `alerts` table, and
settings `tz_offset_minutes`, `overdue_alerts_enabled`, `overdue_grace_minutes`,
`overdue_repeat_hours`, `room_lock_on_checkout`, `room_lock_grace_minutes`,
`room_lock_message`.
