## v1.7.0 — Printable room sheets

For guests who can't or won't scan a QR code, every room can now be printed as
an A4 sheet carrying **the QR code and the same details in plain text**.

### What's on a sheet
Logo and property name, room number and name, the welcome message, the room's
QR code with its link printed underneath, and then in large type: Wi-Fi network,
Wi-Fi password, check-out time, reception number, restaurant name and number,
menu address, emergency number and any room notes. Reception number repeats in
the footer.

### Printing
**QR Codes tab:**
- `Print sheet` on any room card — one page for that room.
- `Print all room sheets` — one A4 page per room in a single job.
- `Print sheet` on the arrival check-in QR — a reception/entrance sheet with a
  "can't scan?" note.

Also on the Rooms tab, from the room's QR button.

Before printing you can tick off:
- the **QR code** (details only — for a guest with no camera)
- the **printed details** (QR only — a clean code card for the room)
- the **Wi-Fi password** (leave it off for a sheet displayed in public areas)

### Notes
- Sheets open in a new tab at `/print/rooms`, `/print/room/{code}` and
  `/print/checkin`, and pull their data using the console session, so a guest
  who guesses the address gets nothing.
- Needs the *View & print QR codes and room sheets* permission (renamed), so
  staff can't print Wi-Fi passwords unless you allow it.
- Laid out for A4 portrait with a page break between rooms; readable on a phone
  too if you'd rather send it than print it.

### API
- `GET /api/print/rooms` and `GET /api/print/room/{code}` — sheet data
  including the QR image path, room URL and logo, regardless of whether the
  room is currently occupied
