## v1.4.2 — Pick an existing contact on manual check-in

- **Manual check-in now starts with a contact picker.** Search saved guests by
  name, phone, email or ID; the eight most recent contacts are listed as soon
  as the box is focused. Clicking one fills in name, phone, email and ID.
- Each result shows how many previous stays the guest has and the date of the
  last one, and flags anyone who is already checked in somewhere so you don't
  double-book them.
- The selected contact appears as a chip (with previous-stay count, last stay
  and vehicle reg) and can be cleared with ✕. Typing a different name over a
  picked contact detaches it and creates a fresh record instead.
- The stay is now bound to the chosen guest by ID, so returning guests never
  get a duplicate record — even when they have no ID, email or phone saved.

### Fix
- Checking in a returning guest through the manual form used to blank out their
  saved **address** and **vehicle registration**, because the quick form doesn't
  collect those fields. Blank fields no longer overwrite what is already on
  record.

### API
- `GET /api/guests` — now returns `visits`, `last_stay` and `in_house` per
  guest, and accepts `?limit=`
- `POST /api/stays` — accepts an optional `guest_id` to bind the stay to an
  existing guest record
